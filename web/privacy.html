Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to process your bus bookings. This includes your name, age, gender, email address, and phone number. This data is securely stored in our local databases.

2. How We Use Data

Your contact details are used to send e-tickets, payment confirmations, and SMS updates regarding bus delays or boarding point changes. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

3. Data Sharing

We share exactly the necessary passenger details with the bus operators so they can verify your identity during boarding.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your account passwords and transaction histories.
